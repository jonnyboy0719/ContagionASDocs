**Format examples**    
`A` - **Short YYYY-MM-DD date, equivalent to Y-B-C**    
`B` - **Month as a decimal number (01-12)**    
`C` - **Day of the month, zero-padded (01-31)**    
`D` - **Day of the month (1-31)**    
`E` - **Hour in 24h format (0-23)**    
`F` - **Minute (0-59)**    
`G` - **Second (0-59)**    
`M` - **Month as a number**    
`T` - **time representation**    
`Y` - **Week-based year**
